Jo Guldi

Jo Guldi (PhD History, UC Berkeley, '08) is a member of the Harvard Society of Fellows and teaches digital history at the University of Chicago. Her first book, The Necessity for Infrastructure (Harvard University Press, 2011) documents the rise of the modern infrastructure state in the building of Britain's interkingdom highway network between 1790 and 1835.

Brian Croxall

During #geoinst, Brian Croxall was a Visiting Assistant Professor of English at Clemson University. Brian received his Ph.D. in 2008 from Emory University.

Thomas Finger

Thomas Finger is a Ph.D. candidate in the Corcoran Department of History at the University of Virginia. Tom specializes in U.S. environmental history and the history of technology.

Barbara Hui

During #geoinst, Barbara Hui was a PhD candidate in Comparative Literature at UCLA where she completed a dissertation entitled Narrative Networks: Mapping Literature at the Turn of the 21st Century.
